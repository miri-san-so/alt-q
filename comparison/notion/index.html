Alt+Q

2023

Simple alternative to Notion

No more pausing podcast/video to write down your thoughts. Just press Alt+Q and write it down and it wont bother you anymore.

Problems I face with notion

  • Notion is a powerful tool, but it can be overwhelming to use
  • Dumping ideas into notion feels wrong
  • Its slow af, takes so much time to load

How Alt+Q tries to address it?

  • Instanly opens by Alt+Q shortcut
  • Type & Enter to save into a simple text file on your computer
  • No pressure to organize

Who should use Alt+Q?

  • Its not a note-taking tool but a thought capturing tool
  • Anyone who just needs a simple note taking tool without the pressure to organize the tool

Which features are coming to Alt+Q?

  • HTML option to view the text file
  • Quick Audio Recording
Made with love for simple things
